Summary: We are combining two movie datasets to focus on cinematic reviews and overall box office. We want to create a table comparing high movie review scores to their box office revenue. 

Extract: Using two csv datasets from kaggle.com. 

Transform: Join the two datasets and create a name using SQL. We will also perform a data cleaning. 

Load: It will be loaded into a relational database.
